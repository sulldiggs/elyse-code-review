Follow RunKeeper ambassador Simon as he runs from Boston to NYC, only to complete the TCS New York City Marathon!

Simon is a U.K.-based ultramarathoner who lost his sight at the age of 17 and has used RunKeeper to safely run on the open roads. With the help of Airbnb, he's coming to the U.S. for his first official marathon and starting in RunKeeper's hometown of Boston. Check in each day to see how he's progressing, which Airbnb home he's resting up at, and how you can join him.
